Glad to meet you here! I am Zhoujun Cheng, a first-year Master student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) guided by Prof. Fan Cheng. Before that, I received the B.E. degree in IEEE honor class (computer science) at SJTU. I spent time as a research intern at MVIG guided by Prof. Cewu Lu.

My research interests currently lie within structured data(tabular data) modeling and pretrained language models, aiming to explore the gap between structured data and natural language. I am now working remotely in Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) DKI group guided by Haoyu Dong.
